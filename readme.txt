Usage Tips for HR Teams

For Best Results: Upload text-based resumes (DOCX preferred over PDF)
Job Matching: Always include job descriptions for accurate matching scores
Bulk Processing: Process multiple candidates and compare results
Report Generation: Use the PDF export for stakeholder meetings
Interview Preparation: Focus on the suggested interview questions and areas

This enhanced system provides a comprehensive, visually stunning, and professionally-focused tool that HR teams will love to use for candidate assessment and decision-making!
